Compliance for Building Software

1
Business and product decisions are compliance decisions
The choices that shape the architecture the most:
  • What data you collect, and how sensitive it is
  • Where your users are, which jurisdictions apply
  • Who you share data with, third parties & suppliers
  • How you use it, storage, profiling & AI
2
Know your data. Know your suppliers.
For most applications, the baseline that satisfies most of every law is the same:
  • Inventory & classify every data type you collect
  • Map every vendor & partner that touches that data
  • Lock each down with a data-processing / sharing agreement
  • Inform users, secure the data, log access, notify on breach
3
Leverage AI for Regulatory Analysis
  • Identify the relevant laws from your geography & jurisdiction
  • Map your application (PRD & features) to those laws
  • Integrate a regulatory agent into the development workflow
