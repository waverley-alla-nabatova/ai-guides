💳 Bank-Card Spending

The Data
Financial Data
Bank-card transactions & spending, pulled via account linking. Reveals income, habits, health, and lifestyle.
Sensitive · EU + US
→
① What the Law Expects
EU · GDPR
Spending data is personal data · needs a lawful basis + security
EU · PSD2
Accessing bank data needs licensed access + strong authentication
California · CCPA / CPRA
Financial account data is Sensitive PI · opt-in + limit use
US · GLBA
Financial data needs a written security-safeguards program
Industry · PCI DSS
Card data must meet strict security standards
→
② What We Build
🔢Never store raw card numbers (tokenize)
🏦Use a licensed aggregator, not raw bank logins
🔐Encrypt financial data end-to-end
🎚️"Limit use" control (California)
👁️Strict access controls + audit logs
🚨Fraud & breach detection + notification
⚠️ Spending data is more than money A transaction history quietly reveals health (pharmacies, clinics), beliefs, vices, and relationships. Treat it as sensitive even though it looks like "just numbers."
